Tower Manufacture

Cell towers, also known as cell sites, are where electric communications equipment and antennae are mounted, allowing the surrounding area to use wireless communication devices like telephones and radios.

Site Survey

Site survey is needed to insure that the location for cell tower is perfect or not. A tower takes up to two weeks to be completed and some time even more; depending the site and the height of the telecommunication towers.

Civil Construction

Construction is a general term meaning the art and science to form objects, systems, or organizations, and comes from Latin construction and Old French construction. To construct is the verb: the act of building, and the noun is construction: how something is built, the nature of its structure.

Tower Installation

Cell tower have variety of heights. We install 3 meter, 6 meter, 9 meter, 15 meter, 20 meter, 25 meter, 40 meter towers.

DG (Diesel Generator)

Diesel Generator is used to generate energy to function the tower equipment. They are a vital part of cell tower. As you know by its name, DG's are diesel based electricity generators.
